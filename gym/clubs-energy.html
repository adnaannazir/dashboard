Energy Club



As a part of the technical council of IIT Mandi, this club plays a cruical role in the field of mechanical engineering. It focuses on developing green and effective energy methods.It organizes activities like arCAD, MechFest.This club was instrumental in setting up of SAE collegiate society at IIT Mandi.

SUPRA SAEINDIA is an exciting competition which presents the Engineering Students with a challenging task of designing, simulating and modeling a formula type race car. Each team designs and fabricates a prototype based on the strict guidelines provided to them and the car should perform in a dynamic event in a live race track
